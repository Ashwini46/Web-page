About US

Our Company

Our primary customer is always the consultant. By staying focused on helping consultants be successful at delivering success to both their customers and the firms they work for, we keep ourselves true to the fact that project success comes when all elements are balanced. This approach has led us to work with many like-minded firms including companies. Our warmest thanks goes out to these companies for challenging themselves to do something different. It isn't easy to take a leap of faith when you run a P&L worth hundreds of millions of dollars and start tackling the big problems, so Voller Biz-Tech wants to recognize both their success and the courage it took for them to earn it!

Our Goal and Values

Voller Biz-Tech was born from the frustration felt by consultants while trying to achieve the sometimes conflicting goals of their customers and their firm. Originally, our goal was to give consultants the complete foundation for what they needed to be successful in the project environment. But of course, there are two more important entities in this endeavor for project success. So over time, our goal has become to simply help all parties involved in the delivery of technology projects to achieve a higher rate of success.
